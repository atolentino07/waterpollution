Government laws / activities

This list contains laws/activities made by the national and international government to answer water pollution

Philippine Clean Water Act of 2004 (R.A. 9275)

aims to protect, preserve and revive all bodies of water in the Philippines. This was also made to have a proper quality monitoring water rehabilitation programs and education about water pollution to conserve the Philippine waters.

World Water Vision 2025

also known as Vision 21 was conceived during the Global Forum on Water Supply and Sanitation held in Manila in 1997 to improve universal access to water supply and sanitation facilities through shared efforts.

Advance Training Modules for Pollution Control Officers (PCOs)(MC No. 2019-004)

contains update on Clean Water Act and an overview for Wastewater Treatment, Flow Measurement, Collection and Disposal Systems; Basic Wastewater Treatment Practices: Physical, Biological and Chemical.
